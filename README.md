# Bon-Ton Stores

The Bon-Ton Stores was a regional department store chain in the United States that operated stores across the Midwest, Northeast, and upper Great Plains. Founded in 1898 in York, Pennsylvania, the company sold apparel, accessories, cosmetics, home furnishings, and other merchandise. The company filed for bankruptcy and ceased operations in 2018.

## Store Banners

The Bon-Ton Stores operated under multiple regional banners:

- **Bon-Ton** - Primary brand banner
- **Bergner's** - Illinois-based stores
- **Boston Store** - Wisconsin-based stores
- **Carson's** - Midwest stores
- **Elder-Beerman** - Ohio and surrounding states
- **Herberger's** - Upper Midwest stores
- **Younkers** - Iowa and surrounding states

## Status

The Bon-Ton Stores filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection in February 2018 and subsequently liquidated all store locations. The brand and intellectual property were acquired after the bankruptcy proceedings.
